Enhance the CyberVerse system by adding a full Storyline Campaign Mode with chapters, narrative progression, and AI-generated story missions.

DO NOT rebuild the app. Extend the existing mission, AI, and progression systems.

---

1. CORE CONCEPT

* Introduce a Story Mode where players progress through a cyber narrative
* Story is divided into Chapters → Missions → Objectives
* Each player experiences the story differently based on:

  * hackerType (Defender / Attacker)
  * skill tree
  * decisions made

---

2. STORY STRUCTURE

Create Chapters:

Chapter 1: “The Breach Begins”

* Intro to cyber world
* Basic missions

Chapter 2: “Shadow Network”

* Hidden attackers emerge

Chapter 3: “System Collapse”

* Large-scale attacks

Chapter 4: “Counterstrike Protocol”

* Player fights back

Chapter 5: “Final Firewall”

* Ultimate challenge

---

3. BRANCHING STORY PATHS

* Defender Storyline:

  * Focus on protection, investigation, recovery

* Attacker Storyline:

  * Focus on infiltration, exploitation, domination

* Player choices affect:

  * Mission types
  * Difficulty
  * Rewards

---

4. AI-DRIVEN STORY MISSIONS

* AI generates missions WITH narrative context

Input to AI:

* Chapter number
* Player role
* Skills
* Previous choices

AI Output:
{
title: "Mission Title",
storyText: "Narrative scene",
objectives: [],
choices: [
"Option A",
"Option B"
],
consequences: {},
rewards: {}
}

---

5. PLAYER CHOICES & CONSEQUENCES

* Add decision points:

  * Example:
    “Block attack immediately” OR “Trace attacker”

* Each choice:

  * Changes next mission
  * Affects difficulty
  * Alters rewards

6. STORY PROGRESSION SYSTEM

Database:
Add:

* currentChapter

  * completed missions

* End of each chapter:

  * Boss challenge

Examples:

* Defender → stop massive cyber attack

* Attacker → break high-security system

* Higher rewards

9. REWARD SYSTEM

* Completing chapters gives:

  * XP boost

10. IMMERSION FEATURES

11. FINAL GOAL

The game should feel like a cinematic cybersecurity journey where:

* Players are part of a larger story
